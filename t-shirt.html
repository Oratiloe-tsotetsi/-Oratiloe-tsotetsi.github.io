Product Details

A cotton t-shirt is a basic garment typically made from cotton fabric, known for its softness, comfort, and breathability.
It features a simple design, often with short sleeves and a crew neckline, although variations in neckline
and sleeve length exist. Cotton t-shirts are versatile and can be found in various colors, patterns, and styles, suitable
for both casual and semi-casual wear. They are popular for their ease of care and ability to withstand frequent washing,
making them a staple in many people's wardrobes.
